About

My name is Tom Power. I’m from Dingle, Co. Kerry on the west coast of Ireland but I now spend most of my time in Dublin studying, composing, writing and coding. I have a degree in Theoretical Physics, that one is a long story. Ask me about it sometime. 

I’m a graduate of the wonderful Master's in Music and Media Technology TCD. I have a Senior Certificate degree in Piano from the Royal Irish Academy of Music.

I’ve been writing space/progressive rock music as Wild Surmise for the past seven years, releasing 10 albums. A nice blend of influences from Tool, Rush, Steve Reich, Philip Glass and Tycho. I've been writing experimental electronic music as L'Homme Moyen. One of my proudest moments to date is my "Eternal/Ephemeral" project. I wrote a six track album which corresponds to a six chapter novella.

Please do not hesitate to get in touch. If you would like to license some music or would like to discuss some custom music for your project, send a carrier pigeon or white raven to: tothepoweroftom@gmail.com
