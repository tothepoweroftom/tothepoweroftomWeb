Thomas Power

Composer, Web Developer, Music Technologist

The intersection of music, code and technology.

I have been a lot of things in life. A theoretical physicist, a piano player in a bar, a co-founder and front end developer for a startup, a baker, a film composer, a web developer and a music technologist. I write music as Wild Surmise and L'Homme Moyen. I've scored short films and ads. I've written a novella and a few short stories. And I've developed websites and algorithms.

I don't believe in right/left brain dominance. I try and balance my mathematical and analytical mind with my creative and musical mind — tying together my Bachelor's degree in Theoretical Physics and Master's degree in Music & Media Technologies from Trinity College Dublin.

Moving forward I plan on pursuing my music while at the same time improving my programming skills. They are the two strands in the helical coil that is my DNA!
